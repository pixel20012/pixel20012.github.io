## Private Use

## Definitions

For purposes of this Addendum, the following terms apply. "Derivative Work" carries the meaning established in Article 4 of the OPL.

**"Explicit Sexual Content"** means any depiction of sexual acts, sexual intercourse, or graphic display of genitalia in a sexual context.

**"Mature Content"** means depictions of non-explicit nudity, suggestive sexual themes, or sexualized imagery that does not constitute Explicit Sexual Content. Romantic or affectionate depictions that do not include nudity or sexual suggestion are not Mature Content for purposes of this Addendum.

**"Graphic Violence"** means depictions of extreme physical harm, torture, mutilation, or gore that a reasonable person would find grossly offensive and that serve no narrative, educational, or artistic purpose.

**"Mature Violence"** means depictions of significant physical harm, realistic injury detail, or substantial blood that do not rise to the level of Graphic Violence. Depictions of action, fantasy combat, or mild violence that a reasonable person would not find grossly offensive are not Mature Violence for purposes of this Addendum.

**"Political Campaign Use"** means the use of Assets or Derivative Works in materials produced for, distributed by, or on behalf of any electoral campaign, political action committee, ballot measure campaign, or candidate for public office, where such use is intended to promote, oppose, or influence a candidate, officeholder, or ballot measure. The following are not Political Campaign Use for purposes of this Addendum: personal political expression by individual Users; journalistic or documentary coverage of political subjects; educational or commentary content regarding political topics.
