## Folder Overview

### `cc-licensed/`
Assets sourced from creators under a Creative Commons license. Permitted use varies by license variant — always check the specific subfolder or file attribution for the applicable CC type (CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-NC, etc.) before use in client or commercial work.

### `opl-media/`
Assets released under the Open Public License or equivalent open licensing frameworks. Generally permissive but may carry attribution or share-alike requirements. Treat similarly to `cc-licensed/` — verify specific terms before commercial use.

### `pixel20012/`
Original works authored by pixel20012. Full copyright retained unless otherwise noted. Includes photography, graphic design, and other self-produced creative assets.

Subdirectory structure:
```
pixel20012/
└── photography/
    └── vrchat/
        ├── portfolio/
        │   ├── portraits/
        │   ├── events/
        │   └── worlds/
        └── commissions/
            └── [client-alias]/
                ├── finals/
                └── raws/
```

### `proprietary/`
Assets that are owned by a third party and subject to restrictive licensing. May include purchased assets, platform-granted badges, or assets tied to a specific EULA. Do not redistribute. Verify terms before any use outside personal/display contexts.

### `web/`
Assets sourced from the web with assumed-free or unverified permissive status. License terms have not been formally confirmed. Use with caution — not cleared for commercial use by default. Audit before including in client deliverables or commercial projects.
